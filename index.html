Himachal Pradesh offers a diverse range of landscapes, from the lush green valleys of Kullu and Kangra to the stark desert-like terrain of Lahaul and Spiti. It's a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Attractions

Main Attractions

  1. Shimla:The capital city of Himachal Pradesh, Shimla is famous for its colonial architecture, scenic beauty, and pleasant climate. Must-visit places include The Ridge, Mall Road, Jakhoo Temple, and the Viceregal Lodge.
  2. Kullu: The Kullu Valley is renowned for its lush greenery and adventure activities. Visit Kullu town, enjoy white-water rafting on the Beas River, and explore Naggar Castle.
  3. Great Himalayan National Park: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this park is a biodiversity hotspot with a variety of flora and fauna. It's a haven for trekkers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Local Favourites

  1. Dal Lake, Dharamshala: While smaller than its famous Kashmir namesake, Dal Lake in Dharamshala is a serene spot for boating and enjoying the natural beauty.
  2. Chail Cricket Ground: Situated at an altitude of 2,144 meters, this cricket ground is the highest in the world. It's a favorite among cricket enthusiasts and offers breathtaking views.
  3. Rani Sui Lake, Manali: A pristine glacial lake surrounded by lush green meadows, Rani Sui Lake is a favorite spot for trekkers and nature lovers.
